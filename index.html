"Titanic" Movie Review

Title description, Dec 7, 2017

Titanic tells the love story between two boat passengers, Jack and Rose. Jack comes from a poor background, oppositely Rose is from a wealthy family who does not approve of Jack due to his background abs social status. The two fall in love as the boat embarks on it's journey, before it is interrupted by an iceburg, causing the boat to begin to sink. Jack ultimately falls for Rose, allowing her to live her life, by sacrificing his own life.

"The Way I Used to be" Book Review

Title description, Sep 2, 2017

The Way I Used to Be, tells the story of Eden as she goes through 4 years of high school battling trauma and friendship difficulties. During the winter break of her freshman year, Eden is sexually assaulted by her older brother's best friend, Kevin. Kevin was described as like family to Eden, someone she could trust, someone she saw a lot. The book deals with the difficulties of post-assault trauma, and how it can affect a person, this person in specific being Eden.
